NASA, SpaceX Clear Demo-2 Mission for Launch on Wednesday

NASA and SpaceX on Friday gave a "go" for the Demo-2 mission, the first launch of astronauts on an American rocket from American soil since the last space shuttle mission in 2011. Launch of the mission, dubbed Launch America, is scheduled for 4:33 p.m. EDT on Wednesday, May 27. Members of the public are invited to participate in the mission through NASA's virtual guest operations and to apply for virtual NASA social.
